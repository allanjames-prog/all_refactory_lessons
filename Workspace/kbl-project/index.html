Director
Access Rights:
  • View total sales and aggregated reports from all branches.
  • Monitor overall business performance without editing privileges.
  • Cannot view individual sales records, only aggregated figures.
Restrictions:
  • Cannot record sales or procurements.
  • Cannot modify product prices or inventory.
Manager – Oversees Branch Operations
Access Rights:
  • Can record produce procurement (new stock entries).
  • Can set product prices for items.
  • Can make sales at the branch level.
  • Can view stock levels and receive out-of-stock alerts.
  • Can see all transactions made within their branch.
Restrictions:
  • Cannot modify sales made by other agents.
  • Cannot access sales data from other branches.
  • Cannot view overall business financials (only the director can).
Sales Agent – Handles Day-to-Day Sales
Access Rights:
  • Can record sales transactions at their respective branch.
  • Can record credit sales for trusted buyers.
  • Can view stock levels (to check availability before selling).
Restrictions:
  • Cannot record new stock entries (only managers can).
  • Cannot set or change prices (prices are pre-determined by the manager).
  • Cannot view aggregated sales reports (only the director can).
  • Can only access records specific to their branch.
Business Rules Summary:
  1. Stock is tracked automatically, and when an item is out of stock, the manager is notified.
  2. Only managers can add new stock (sales agents cannot).
  3. Sales agents can only sell pre-priced items set by the manager.
  4. Director can only view aggregated sales reports, not individual transactions.
  5. Credit sales must include buyer details (NIN, location, phone, etc.).
